Percussion

After four years on the Pioneer High School Drumline, which culminated in my election to the position of center snare, I decided to return as an instructor. I specialized as the Snare Tech (pictured with the snare section above) and spent the entire summer with an incredible group of students. You can find part of their final performance here, or watch the master himself here. I am currently a member of the Duke University Marching Band (DUMB), playing snare on the drumline. The biggest perk of this position: basketball season. I am fortunate enough to play drum set for all of the home games in Cameron Indoor, which has resulted in the witnessing of some unbelievable games.
